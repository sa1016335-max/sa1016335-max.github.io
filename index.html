About Me

I am an M.Sc. graduate in Economics from HSE University (Moscow) and a Fulbright Alumnus. My research focuses on Development Economics and the Economics of Education, specifically using applied econometrics to evaluate policy interventions.

Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Centre for Economic Research in Pakistan (CERP) and as a Research Intern at IFAT (Hungary). I have extensive experience with large-scale household datasets, RCT implementation, and causal inference using Stata and R.
